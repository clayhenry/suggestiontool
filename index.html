Cool but, What is a hashtag?

Simply put, a hashtag is a link that people, like you, can group posts on social media (such as Instagram, Twitter, Facebook and Pinterest).

This link is automatically created by the platform anytime you append # (hash symbol) in front of a keyword.

This group is based on common interest the post is based on, for example #giveaways. When clicking on this hashtag on Twitter, you will get the latest tweets that relate to giveaways.

When you're crafting a post for any social media platform, you want to reach widest and most relevant audience that have common interest . As hashtags themselves have a followings and trends, it can be tricky creating the most effective tags.

You can hashtag any word in your post, what words you tag can effect the engagement of your audience. It can't be too broad, too narrow (unless you're targeting a specific group), or a tag that is overused.

Most times, what hashtags you choose in your post will make a substantial difference in how your post will be received and the engagement you get.
